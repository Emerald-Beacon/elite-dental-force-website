EDiFi Enterprise
VS
Weave

Insurance Revenue Recovery
vs Patient Communication

Weave is a best in class patient communication and engagement platform. EDiFi is a dental revenue intelligence platform. These tools solve fundamentally different problems, which is why leading practices run both.

The honest answer: these platforms are not competitors. Weave serves patients. EDiFi recovers insurance revenue from payers.
Role Clarity

Two Different Jobs. Both Critical.

If you are comparing Weave and EDiFi, you may be solving the wrong problem. Here is what each platform actually does.

EDiFi Enterprise

Insurance Revenue Intelligence

EDiFi works between your practice and insurance payers. It verifies eligibility before appointments, scrubs claims before submission, audits every payment against contracted rates, recovers underpayments, and manages denial appeals.

  • Real time eligibility verification
  • AI powered claim scrubbing
  • Payment audit vs fee schedules
  • Underpayment detection and recovery
  • Denial command center and AI appeals
  • Medical billing crossover (CDT to CPT)
  • DSO multi location revenue orchestration
Weave

Patient Communication and Engagement

Weave works between your practice and your patients. It handles phone systems, two way texting, appointment reminders, online reviews, patient forms, and patient payment collection (not insurance payments).

  • VoIP phone system for dental offices
  • Two way patient texting
  • Appointment reminder automation
  • Online review management
  • Digital intake and patient forms
  • Patient payment collection (not insurance)
  • Schedule fill and recall campaigns
