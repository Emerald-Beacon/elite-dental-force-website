Why EDiFi Wins

Three Capabilities Dentrix RCM Does Not Have

Payment Audit Engine

EDiFi's payment audit engine compares every insurance payment against your contracted fee schedules and flags underpayments, phantom write offs, and EOB discrepancies automatically. Dentrix RCM has no equivalent capability. $59K was found in a single beta validation.

Medical Billing Crossover

EDiFi identifies dental procedures billable to medical insurance and generates CMS-1500 forms with full CDT to CPT crosswalk. Dentrix RCM is dental only. Practices using EDiFi access $80K to $200K in new annual medical reimbursement most competitors leave untouched.

Voice AI and Real Time Eligibility

Bella AI handles inbound calls, outbound payer calls for eligibility, and voice driven perio charting chairside. Dentrix RCM relies on manual eligibility checks via batch clearinghouse queries. EDiFi runs real time checks, not overnight batch files.

The Core Difference

Purpose Built vs Bolted On

Dentrix Revenue Cycle Management was designed as an add-on to the Dentrix PMS. It improves existing workflows but is constrained by what the PMS can see. EDiFi was built from the ground up as an AI intelligence layer that works across any data source.

AI learns your payer patterns over time
EDiFi's denial prediction and appeal engine learns which arguments win with each payer and which procedure combinations trigger reviews. Dentrix RCM applies static rules that do not adapt.
Works with your existing PMS, not around it
If you switch from Dentrix to Open Dental or Eaglesoft, EDiFi comes with you. Dentrix RCM is locked to Dentrix. For DSOs managing mixed PMS portfolios, this is a critical constraint.
Finds money Dentrix RCM does not look for
The entire payment audit category, underpayment detection, and EOB reconciliation are capabilities Dentrix RCM does not offer. In beta validation, 27% of submitted claims had payment discrepancies worth $59K for a single practice.
Medical billing opens an entirely new revenue channel
Dentrix RCM does not support medical billing crossover. EDiFi identifies procedures billable to medical insurance at 40% to 200% higher reimbursement and handles the full CMS-1500 workflow. This alone can add $80K to $200K annually per practice.
