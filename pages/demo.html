EDiFi End to End:
Patient Scheduled to Revenue Recovered

Watch EDiFi handle the complete dental revenue cycle in six scenes. Real platform. Real workflows. No actors, no mockups.

Morning Huddle, Automated. EDiFi's dashboard shows every patient on today's schedule with real-time eligibility status. Green means confirmed and covered. Yellow means a coverage question needs resolution before the appointment. Red means do not treat without verification. Your front desk knows exactly what to expect before the first patient walks in.

Coverage Confirmed Before They Arrive. EDiFi verifies insurance coverage in real time — not the night before, not at check-in, but automatically as soon as the appointment is scheduled. Benefit breakdowns, deductibles, frequencies, and missing tooth clauses are all surfaced and documented before the patient walks through the door.

Claims Built from Clinical Data, Not Memory. After treatment, EDiFi's AI claim scrubber reads the clinical documentation and auto-assigns CDT procedure codes and ICD-10 diagnosis codes. Every claim is formatted to that specific payer's submission requirements. Coding errors, missing modifiers, and invalid code combinations are caught before submission — not after denial.

Every Payment Audited Against Your Fee Schedule. When the insurance EOB arrives, EDiFi's Payment Audit Engine automatically compares what was paid against your contracted rate for every procedure, every payer, every time. This is the step that found $59,000 in underpayments across 2,399 claims in a single practice — discrepancies a billing team never had time to catch manually.

Underpayments Flagged. Appeals Automated. Every discrepancy is classified as Collectible, Appealable, or Adjustable and routed to the right action queue. EDiFi generates appeal letters with clinical context specific to the denial reason and the payer's known objections. The appeal engine learns which arguments win — improving recovery rates over time.

Revenue Recovered. Full Visibility. The analytics dashboard shows exactly how much was recovered, which payers are underpaying systematically, which procedures are most frequently denied, and where your billing team should focus next. This is the intelligence layer that turns your revenue cycle from a cost center into a competitive advantage.

What You Just Saw

Six Workflows. One Platform.

Every scene in this tour is a real EDiFi feature in production use with our beta practices today.

Scene 1
Morning Huddle Dashboard

AI generated daily briefing with patient eligibility status, production targets, unscheduled treatment, and flags for the front desk team before the day starts.

Scene 2
Real Time Eligibility Verification

Live payer API queries return coverage details, benefit breakdowns, deductibles, frequencies, and authorization requirements before the appointment.

Scene 3
AI Claim Scrubbing

Clinical notes are parsed, CDT and ICD-10 codes are assigned by AI, and every claim is validated against that specific payer's formatting rules before submission.

Scene 4
Payment Audit Engine

Every EOB payment is compared against contracted fee schedules. Underpayments, phantom write-offs, and EOB discrepancies are flagged automatically. $59K found in beta validation.

Scene 5
Denial Command Center

Denied and underpaid claims are classified, prioritized, and routed. AI generates appeal letters with clinical context specific to the payer and denial reason.

Scene 6
Revenue Intelligence Analytics

Cross-practice analytics show recovery rates, payer performance, denial patterns, and where revenue is being systematically lost — so you can fix the root cause, not just the symptom.
