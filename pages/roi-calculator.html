Methodology

How We Calculate Your Recovery

Every number in this calculator is anchored to real data from EDiFi's beta validation, not industry averages or vendor marketing claims.

Underpayment Recovery Rate
EDiFi's payment audit engine finds approximately 5.47% of your total insurance billing volume in underpayments (validated: $59K / 2,399 claims at ~$450 avg claim). The calculator applies an 87% recovery efficiency based on beta outcomes.
Denial Recovery Increment
Most practices recover 5% to 8% of denied claims manually. EDiFi's AI appeal engine targets an additional 10% recovery on top of your current baseline, using clinical context and payer specific argument libraries.
EDiFi Investment Model
EDiFi is priced at $499 per location per month. The ROI and payback calculations use this baseline. Enterprise and DSO pricing varies. Contact sales for multi location pricing that reflects your specific portfolio size.
Important Limitations
Results vary significantly by payer mix, procedure mix, geographic market, and existing billing team capability. This calculator is designed to illustrate potential, not guarantee specific outcomes. Book a demo to run the analysis on your actual claims data.
