How It Works

Revenue Intelligence Across the Entire Patient Journey

EDiFi runs in the background of your practice — automating every revenue touchpoint from the moment an appointment is booked to the day a payment is reconciled.

Before the Appointment
STEP 01

Patient Scheduled

EDiFi connects to your PMS. When an appointment is booked, patient and insurance data flows into the platform automatically — no manual entry needed.

PMS Auto-Import
STEP 02

Eligibility Verified

EDiFi submits a real-time eligibility check 24 to 72 hours before the appointment. Coverage gaps, benefit limits, and remaining maximums are flagged immediately.
