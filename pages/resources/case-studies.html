Beta Validation — Payment Audit Engine

Single Location Practice Discovers $59,000 in Underpaid Claims

Single Location Practice Utah Dentrix PMS Beta Validation

This practice had been operating with an experienced in-house billing coordinator for years. Claims were submitted on time, EOBs were reviewed, and the practice believed they had a competent revenue cycle. No one had ever systematically audited whether they were being paid correctly.

EDiFi's Payment Audit Engine ingested 2,399 historical claims and compared each insurance payment against the practice's contracted fee schedules. The audit ran automatically. No additional staff time was required. Results were available within hours.

1
Claims ingested: 2,399 submitted claims were imported from Dentrix into EDiFi's audit engine.
2
Fee schedule matching: Each payment was compared against contracted rates for the specific procedure and payer combination.
3
Discrepancy classification: 647 claims were flagged with payment shortfalls ranging from minor rounding errors to significant systematic underpayments.
4
Recovery routing: Each discrepancy was classified as Collectible, Appealable, or Adjustable with priority routing for maximum recovery.
