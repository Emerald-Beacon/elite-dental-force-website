What Our BAA Covers

Our BAA is reviewed annually by healthcare compliance attorneys and updated to reflect current HIPAA regulatory guidance. It is included at no additional cost with every EDiFi subscription.

  • PHI permitted uses and disclosure restrictions
  • Breach notification within 72 hours of discovery
  • Subcontractor and sub-processor chain of trust
  • Access to your PHI upon request within 30 days
  • Data return or destruction upon contract termination
  • Annual compliance review and agreement update cycle
  • Mutual indemnification for compliance failures
