Your Daily Command Center

The EDiFi dashboard replaces the morning scramble of checking your PMS, payer portals, and spreadsheets. Everything your front desk and billing team needs is surfaced in one real-time view, prioritized by urgency.

Appointments in the next 2 hours with unresolved insurance, flagged eligibility, or billing notes — sorted by time

A/R Aging Alerts

Live PMS Sync

Appointment board updates as your PMS changes — no manual refresh, no missed appointments, no stale data

Claim Intelligence

Submit Clean. Get Paid Fast.

The Claim Scrubber intercepts every claim before it hits the clearinghouse. The AI checks it against payer rules, CDT code requirements, and past denial patterns — then assigns a denial probability score with a fix recommendation.

Pre-Submission Scrub

Every claim is checked before submission — missing tooth numbers, surface codes, frequency limit violations, prior auth requirements

Denial Probability Score

Each flagged claim gets a probability: "87% chance of denial without correction." Fix it once, not after it comes back

Recommended Fix

EDiFi tells you exactly what to change: "Add both number to claim. CDT D2391 requires surface and tooth number"

94.2% Clean Rate

Beta offices on EDiFi submit at 94.2% first-pass acceptance vs. the 75-80% industry average without a scrubber

Bella Works While You're Closed

Live Session Monitor

Watch Bella's active calls in real time — caller intent, current status, confidence score, and session duration displayed as it happens

Full Transcript

Insurance Verification

Perio Charting AI

Chart with Your Voice. Code Automatically.

Hygienists speak. EDiFi listens. Every pocket depth, BOP, furcation, and mobility gets recorded by voice, plotted on the chart, and used to generate a clinical narrative and CDT code suggestions — all before the patient leaves the chair.

Voice-Controlled Charting

Deepgram Nova-3 Medical model trained on dental terminology. Call out tooth numbers, depths, and findings — EDiFi records exactly what you say

Clinical Alert Engine

When BOP and pocket depth cross clinical thresholds, EDiFi flags the site immediately: "Pocket depths 5/4/5 with bleeding on probing. Localized moderate periodontal involvement"

Auto CDT Coding

Based on chart findings, EDiFi recommends D4341, D4342, D0274 — with payer coverage pulled from the patient's active benefits

AI Narrative

A complete clinical narrative is generated and ready for doctor review before the patient leaves — "PERIODONTAL EXAMINATION — Maria Johnson — April 9, 2026"
