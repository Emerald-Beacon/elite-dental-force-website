How It Works

The Complete Revenue Cycle. Automated End to End.

EDiFi handles every step from patient check-in to payment recovery. Nothing falls through the cracks.

1

Verify Eligibility

Before the patient even arrives, EDiFi confirms coverage, benefits, and authorization requirements, eliminating day-of surprises and front-desk delays.

2

Generate Clean Claims

AI auto-assigns CDT procedure codes and ICD-10 diagnosis codes from clinical findings, formatting every claim to payer-specific submission rules before it leaves your office.

3

Audit Every Payment

EDiFi's Payment Audit Engine compares what you were paid against your contracted fee schedules, flagging underpayments, phantom write-offs, and EOB discrepancies automatically.

4

Recover What's Yours

Findings are classified as Collectible, Appealable, or Adjustable and routed automatically. EDiFi's AI generates appeal letters with clinical context, tracks outcomes, and learns which arguments win — so recovery happens without manual work.
